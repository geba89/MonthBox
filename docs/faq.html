General

What is MonthBox?
MonthBox is a simple monthly budget tracker for iPhone. Add your income, track your expenses, mark bills as paid, and see how much money you have left. That's it. No complicated features, no learning curve.
Do I need to link my bank account?
No. MonthBox is completely manual. You add your income and expenses yourself. We never ask for your bank credentials and have no way to access your accounts. This is by design for your privacy.
Does it work offline?
Yes, completely. MonthBox requires no internet connection to work. All your data is stored locally on your device.

Privacy & Data

Where is my data stored?
All your data is stored locally on your iPhone. We don't have servers, and your financial information never leaves your device (unless you use iCloud backup, which is handled by Apple).
Can I sync across devices?
If you enable iCloud in your iOS settings, your data will automatically sync across your Apple devices. This sync is encrypted and handled entirely by Apple we never see your data.
Can I export my data?
Yes, you can export your budget data to CSV format. This feature allows you to keep backups or analyze your spending in other apps.

Pricing

Is there a subscription?
No. MonthBox is a one-time purchase. Pay once, use forever. No recurring charges, no "premium" upgrades required for basic features.
Why isn't it free?
Free apps typically make money through ads or by selling your data. MonthBox has no ads and collects no data. The one-time purchase keeps the app sustainable without compromising your privacy.
Is there a free trial?
MonthBox offers a free trial period so you can experience the full app before purchasing. Try it for a complete budget cycle to see if it works for you.

Features

How do recurring expenses work?
When you add an expense and mark it as "Fixed Monthly Bill," it will automatically appear in future months. Each month starts with the expense unpaid, ready for you to mark as paid when you pay it.
What's the home screen widget?
The widget shows your remaining budget right on your iPhone's home screen. You can see at a glance how much you have left to spend without opening the app.
Can I track multiple currencies?
MonthBox supports multiple currencies. You can choose your preferred currency in Settings. However, each budget uses a single currency.
What about iPad or Mac support?
MonthBox is currently iPhone-only. iPad and Mac versions may come in future updates based on user demand.

Troubleshooting

My recurring expenses aren't showing up in the new month
Make sure your expenses are marked as "Fixed Monthly Bill" when you create them. Also, try navigating away from the month and back to trigger the auto-population.
I accidentally deleted an expense. Can I recover it?
Unfortunately, deleted expenses cannot be recovered as all data is stored locally. If you have iCloud backup enabled, you may be able to restore from a previous backup.
Will you add [complex feature]?
Probably not. MonthBox is intentionally simple. We focus on doing one thing well: helping you track monthly expenses. We won't add bank linking, investment tracking, or AI predictions. That's the whole point.
