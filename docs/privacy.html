Last updated: December 2024

Simple Version: We don't collect any of your data. Period.

Data Collection

MonthBox does not collect, transmit, or store any personal or financial data on external servers. We have no servers. Your budget data exists only on your device.

Local Storage

All your budget data (expenses, income, settings) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up via iCloud.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud backup in your iOS Settings, your budget data may sync via Apple's iCloud service. In this case:

  • Your data is encrypted and handled by Apple
  • We never see or have access to this data
  • Apple's privacy policy applies to iCloud data

Analytics

MonthBox does not use any analytics services. We don't track how you use the app, what features you use, or how often you open it. We simply don't know.

Third-Party Services

MonthBox contains:

  • No third-party SDKs
  • No advertising networks
  • No tracking pixels
  • No analytics services
  • No crash reporting services that collect personal data

Bank Account Linking

MonthBox does not connect to your bank accounts. All expense and income tracking is manual. We never ask for or have access to your banking credentials.

Data Sharing

Since we don't have your data, we cannot share it. Not with advertisers, not with data brokers, not with anyone. Your financial information stays on your device.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Export: You can export your data as CSV (coming soon)
  • Delete: Delete the app and all your data is gone

Children's Privacy

MonthBox does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app simply doesn't collect data.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Given our commitment to not collecting data, significant changes are unlikely.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@monthbox.app

MonthBox is built by someone who values privacy. Your financial data is yours alone.
